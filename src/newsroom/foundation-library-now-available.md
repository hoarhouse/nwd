The Foundation's resource library is now available, gathering presentations and documents on the ideas at the heart of our work: data sovereignty, sovereign AI, and value-based technology. Each is offered to read online or download in full.

The collection is meant as a working reference — for partners, researchers, and anyone seeking to understand what a more human-centered approach to artificial intelligence looks like in practice. [Browse the library](/library/).
