The Human Communities Platform held its first gathering on 7 May, opening a series of conversations on technology, faith, and the common good. Hosted within a single parish community, the event set the tone for what would follow: a belief that questions about artificial intelligence belong not only to laboratories and boardrooms, but to communities themselves.

It was a modest beginning with a wide ambition — to place ordinary people at the center of decisions about the technologies that increasingly shape their lives. [Read more about the event](/events/may-7-conversation-begins/).
